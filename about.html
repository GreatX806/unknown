About Us

Our Sweet Story

Every great bakery begins with a single moment — a taste that changes everything. For Cakes by Essenx, that moment was the belief that a truly exceptional cake does more than satisfy hunger. It marks milestones, stirs memories, and gives life its essence.

Founded in the heart of Lagos, Nigeria, Cakes by Essenx was born from a deep passion for the art of baking and an uncompromising commitment to quality. What began as a small operation crafting bespoke celebration cakes for friends and family quickly grew into one of Lagos's most trusted names for luxury confectionery and premium event catering.

We specialise in custom-designed celebration cakes that reflect the unique personality and vision of every client — from intimate birthday tiers to grand multi-storey wedding masterpieces. Our repertoire extends beyond cakes to include handcrafted pastries, artisan brownies, fresh donuts, and elegantly presented small chops platters that elevate every gathering.

At Cakes by Essenx, we source only the finest ingredients — rich Belgian-style chocolate, farm-fresh eggs, pure vanilla, and premium local produce — ensuring that every creation that leaves our kitchen is worthy of the occasion it marks. We do not believe in shortcuts. We believe in excellence, delivered with warmth.
